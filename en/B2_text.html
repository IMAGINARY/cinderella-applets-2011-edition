If you combine rotations and scalings the repeated application creates facinating spiral like objects. A simple way to generate such patterns is demonstrated in the program on the right.

One starts with an arbitrary quadrilateral with vertices ABCD. Now one creates a rotated and scaled copy of this quadrilateral such that the edge CD of the old quadrilateral is identified with the edge AB of the new quadrilateral. Then one repeats this process iteratively.

The visualization on the right demonstrates this process starting with an arbitrary quadrilateral whose corners can be freely moved.

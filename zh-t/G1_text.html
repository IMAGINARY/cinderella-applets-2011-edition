Sudoku has gained increasing popularity over the past few years. In a grid of 9 × 9 cells the numbers from 1 to 9 have to be written in a way such that in every row and column each digit occurs exactly once. As if this was not enough also in the highlighted 3 × 3 blocks each digit must occur exactly once.

In a typical Sudoku puzzle a few numbers are already fixed. The puzzle consists of finding an admissible placement of digits for the empty fields. The difficulty ranges from childish to hard as hell.
